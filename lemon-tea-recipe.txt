Ingredients:
    2 tsp lemon juice
    1/2 tsp tea powder
    1 tsp honey
    1 tsp sugar

Directions:
    1. Boil 3 cups of water in a deep saucepan.
    2. Add the tea powder, mix well cook on a medium flame
    for 2 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
    3. Strain the mixture immediately using a strainer and
    discard the tea powder.
    4. Add lemon juice, honey and sugar and mix well.
    5. Pour into 3 individual cups and serve immediately.
